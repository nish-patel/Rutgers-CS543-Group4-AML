The specially marked sphere in the minimap corresponds to a bucket of connected components. The grid map next to it depicts glyphs that can be used to access connected fixed points of specific range of sizes. Each column corresponds to a fixed point value, and each row corresponds to a range of sizes.

The directed meta graph under the minimap encodes a spanning subgraph of the intersection graph of the buckets of connected fixed points. The number of nodes in this meta graph is equal to the number of glyphs in the grid map. The direction of edges is from lower fixed points to higher fixed points.

A particular fixed point can be chosen by click-selecting a glyph in the grid map or the corresponding node in the meta DAG.
